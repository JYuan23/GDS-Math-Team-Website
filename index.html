Whatâ€™s a typical meeting


A typical meeting consists of collaborating with small groups on math problems, potentially engaging with speakers in workshops, and presenting and discussing insightful solutions.

What is expected of club members?


Inside of the official meeting time, we expect members to work together on math problems and be respectful of each other. In addition, members will have the opportunity to attend competitions, but this is not required.

How many hours per week can a member of this club expect to spend on club-related activities?


Committed members of this club will spend anywhere from 1 to 2 hours per week in club-sponsored meetings. Some math competitions will take one to two days, but they usually take place on the weekends.
